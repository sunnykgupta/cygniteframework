Url Helper

How to get Url Segment ?

You can get your url segments as below- 


    http://www.cygniteframework.com/projects/index.php/users/list/23
    http://www.cygniteframework.com/projects/users/list/23

    use Cygnite\Helpers\Url;
    echo Url::segment(2);
Cygnite Framework always counts from your index.php if available in url else it counts from your root path. The above url segment will print "list" as result.

How to get base path of application ?

The method basepath returns your base path of your applications. For example- 

    use Cygnite\Helpers\Url;
    echo Url::basepath();
It returns http://www.cygniteframework.com/projects/ as result.

How to redirect page ?

You can redirect from your page to another - 

   use Cygnite\Helpers\Url;
   Url::redirect_to('Your Url to redirect');

How to refresh/reload the Page ?

You can also reload your page as below- 
   use Cygnite\Helpers\Url;
   Url::redirect_to('users/names','Refresh');

How to encode the url ?

You can use encode method to encode your url- 
   use Cygnite\Helpers\Url;
   Url::encode('your-url-to-encode');

How to decode the url ?

You can use decode method to decode your encoded url- 
   use Cygnite\Helpers\Url;
   Url::decode('your-url-to-decode');
